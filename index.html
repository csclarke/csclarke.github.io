The need for a read and write culture

Free software and the creative commons are the cornerstone to a much needed shift from moving from read only culture to read and write culture. and the Internet core being founded on the core of free software is creating and new cultural paradigm.

A paradigm which is adding value via of collaboration and sharing we are and changing who think about ourselves others and how we transact business. People don't just create thing to get paid as the entry model of copyright and its read only culture contends.They also crate to participate and be become an part then is more then sum total of them selves.
